Our Mission

Moving Pets from Where They're
Overlooked to Where They're Wanted

California's Central Valley — from Bakersfield through Fresno and Modesto — is home to some of the most overcrowded shelters in the state. Dogs and cats sit on death row not because they're unadoptable, but because there's no room and not enough local demand.

Meanwhile, SF Bay Area rescue partners have adoption applications they can't fill. The animals are there. The demand is there. The only thing missing is the transport — and that's exactly what No Paws Left Behind Rescue provides.

We are a transport-only organization. We don't adopt animals out ourselves — we partner with licensed 501(c)3 Bay Area rescue organizations who handle adoptions. Our job is simply to get them there safely.

"There is no better, quicker, more efficient way to save lives than by moving animals to an area where they are in great demand."

— Theresa, Founder

How It Works

The Road to a Forever Home

1

Rescues Choose the Animals

Our Bay Area rescue partners identify the dogs and cats they want to pull — they let us know who needs transport and we coordinate the pickup.

2

We Drive Them North

Dogs and cats are personally transported from Bakersfield, Fresno, and Modesto directly to Bay Area rescue partners — hundreds of miles closer to a home.

3

Partners Find Them Homes

Our Bay Area 501(c)3 rescue partners receive the animals, provide care, and handle all adoptions. We hand off — they find the forever homes.
